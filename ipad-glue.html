Glue and an iPad

- Try to avoid any liquid glues when working on iPads. It'll cause things to not fit. - For a screen to fit right, it needs to be at a correct height or it leaves a gap to the sides.
That is why you should use tape that is designed for that iPad and comes with the display
package. - For touch-ID to work right, it needs to be at a correct height, and liquid glue makes the
button to be constantly in the "pressed" position. So always use for example the glue-tape
which is used to tape the screen on it's place. Just cut perfect squares out of it and it'll
be awesome and durable.
