Face ID

After "Face-ID unavailable" error, it's quite common to lose all hope and
flush the phone from the toilet. Before the toilet manouver, try the following things first: 1. Check the cables and that the front-camera module is properly placed. Try the face-ID
without completely pushing the display in. 2. Check the camera-"holes" of the display that nothing is in the way. 3. Check the shiny dot-projector without touching it (touching may break it.) If it looks
dirty, blow some air on it but don't touch it. 4. Try a new display. 5. Hard reset the phone from DFU-mode. 6. If face-ID is still not working, feel free to flush the phone from the toilet
or use it as a bookmark or something.
